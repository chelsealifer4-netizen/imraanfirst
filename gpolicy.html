MBA & EMBA Policy

  • BUS 500 is a non-credit course for MBA students. However, EMB 500 is a credit course.
  • BUS 698 is a non-credit course. It carries 1 credit only for students admitted before 2020.
  • A student may repeat a course in which the grade is 'B' or lower.
